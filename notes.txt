You cannot aggregate multiple call centers in one call, but you can do it if they
are apart of the same Office

If one office holds multiple call centers (EX: Core institute has appointments and billing, two diff cc)
then data can be grabbed from that one office as a whole without looking at individual call centers.

But, if there were two different call centers across two different offices, two different calls would need
to be made to pull that data together.

So much of this depends on 1. How we want to group call centers and 2. What offices hold these call centers

So there can be a way to provide a list of call centers to aggregate along with the offices they are in,
but that would have to be an argument passed in it is difficult to fully automate.
